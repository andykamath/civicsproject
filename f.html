What lead to America?

So many things led to the creation of the world's strongest democracy. Lack of freedom and fairness caused settlers to come to the new opportunities in America. Soon, however, the French and Indian War caused a ton of expenses on Britain. King George III of Britain, ruling as king at the time, decided that since the war was waged to protect the Americans, that the Americans should pay for the war. While the Americans were fine with this at first, an overwhelming amount of taxation without representation caused unsettlement in the colonies. Americans started to revolt and signed the Declaration of Independence, declaring freedom from England. King George III got enraged and decided it was time for war, thus leading to the American Revolution, where the first battles were at Lexington and Concord (shown below). As we all know, the Colonists won and established the United States

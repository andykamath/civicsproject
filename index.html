What we do

The Technology Student Association's Mission Statement is "Leadership and opportunities in technology, innovation, design and engineering. Members apply STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics) concepts through co-curricular programs". That being said, Providence TSA's mission is to bring Technology and STEM opportunities to students at Providence High School by participating in TSA competitions.
